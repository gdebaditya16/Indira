About us

Indira is a dedicated clothing brand by Debchandrima Singha Roy,an actress and a social-media creator. Indira is Made in Bengal and focuses on the section of "timeless sarees" refering to classic, enduring styles like silk or handloom, known for their elegant, versatile, and culturally significant designs that transcend fleeting fashion trends
